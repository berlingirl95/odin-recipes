Vegetarian Lasanga

Description

This is one tasty, delicious, Garfield-approved vegetarian lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Lasanga sheets
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Mixed Veg
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Chop up the veg into small chunks and cook briefly in the oven until softened.
  2. Add the veg to the tomato sauce.
  3. Layer the lasanga sheets at the bottom of a oven-proof dish.
  4. Add a layer of bechamel sauce on top of the lasanga sheets.
  5. Add a layer of the tomato sauce/mixed veg mixture on top of the bechamel sauce.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until your oven-proof dish is full.
  7. Sprinkle some grated cheese on top.
  8. Put your dish in the oven and cook for 25 mins at 200 degrees C in a fan oven.
  9. Voila! You have a delicious vegetarian lasanga!
