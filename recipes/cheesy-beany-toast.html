Cheesy beany toast

Description

A protein-packed meal for those days when you're feeling too lazy to cook..

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Bread of choice
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put the bread in the toaster.
  2. Place the beans in a pan and cook until hot.
  3. Grate the cheese.
  4. Take the bread from the toaster, and spread a generous amount of butter on the toast.
  5. Pour beans over toast.
  6. Sprinkle grated cheese over beans.
  7. Finish off with a dash of salt and pepper!
