Vegetarian Korma

Description

What could be better than a creamy vegetarian korma? Good for the earth and for your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • Assorted veg
  • Korma sauce
  • Rice
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Chop the veg.
  2. Add veg and korma sauce to a pan and cook until veg is soft.
  3. In a seperate pan, boil the rice.
  4. Dish some of the rice onto a plate, and then dish out the creamy vegetable korma sauce on top.
  5. Place your naan bread in the microwave for a couple of minutes.
  6. Serve the vegetarian korma with the naan bread!
